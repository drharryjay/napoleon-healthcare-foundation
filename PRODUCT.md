# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective donors, partner organizations (churches, Rotary groups, corporates, health institutions, diaspora groups), volunteer health professionals, and community members in Nigeria — most on mid-range Android phones over variable mobile connections. They arrive to answer one of three questions: "Is this NGO real and trustworthy?", "How do I help?", or "What has NHF actually done?"

## Product Purpose

The public website of Napoleon Healthcare Foundation (CAC/IT/NO 85319), a Nigerian community-health NGO delivering free medical outreach, screening, eye/dental care, maternal-child health support, and health education across Delta State and the South-South region. The site exists to establish credibility (verified registration, real outreach reports, real photos), convert visitors into donors/partners/volunteers, and be discoverable in web search. Success = inquiries through the forms, donation contact, and search visibility.

## Brand Personality

Compassionate, credible, grounded. The voice of a working medical team, not a marketing agency. Warm but never sentimental; confident but never boastful. Every claim verifiable — the site deliberately marks unverified figures as pending rather than inventing them.

## Anti-references

- Generic "charity template" sites with stock photos of unnamed people — NHF uses only its own outreach photography.
- Flashy SaaS-style landing pages (gradient text, glassmorphism, hero metrics template).
- Overstated NGO copy ("changing millions of lives") that outruns the verified record.
- Heavy, animation-drenched pages that struggle on mid-range Android devices.

## Design Principles

1. **Proof over polish** — real photos, real numbers, honest "pending" states beat decorative filler.
2. **Calm authority** — restrained motion and color; the work is dramatic, the design doesn't need to be.
3. **Fast on a Lagos bus** — every choice budgeted for mid-range mobile on 3G; performance is part of trust.
4. **One clear next step** — every page ends in donate, partner, or volunteer.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA as the working bar. All motion honors `prefers-reduced-motion`. Touch targets ≥44px. Forms fully keyboard-operable with visible labels. Health content readable at a general-public reading level.
